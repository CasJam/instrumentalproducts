Product planning sprints

Planning a big new feature or the first version of your product? The shaping and planning phase is critical. That's what this service is aimed at.

A typical planning sprint lasts two weeks. During that time, our collaboration will include:

  • A call where I'll ask a ton of questions and gain a solid understanding of the problems we're aiming to solve (and more importantly, why).
  • Async collaboration throughout our sprint
  • Defining the scope and priorities
  • Technical research
  • Functional specs for developers
  • User flows and rough wireframes (where needed)
  • Roadmap planning and milestones

When we wrap, you and your team should have everything you need to start implementing, building and shipping the best version of your product, without delay.

I only book one of these at a time, and typically book out at least a month in advance. Inquire about current availability.
